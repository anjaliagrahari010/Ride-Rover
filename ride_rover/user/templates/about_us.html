How It Works

Using our system, vehicle owners can register as sellers, offering their vehicles for rent, while customers can register as renters to access a wide range of vehicles. Here’s what you need to know:

  • Register and login as a vehicle renter
  • Browse available vehicles or list your own
  • Complete secure booking process
  • Enjoy your rental experience

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the conditions of the vehicle?

Your satisfaction and safety is our priority. We ensure vehicles are well-maintained and ready for your inspection before delivery.

What are the documents required to book the vehicle?

Just Get Driving License, photo and signature for the verification

Is there any distance limit on the vehicle? What if I cross the distance limit ?

No, there is no distance limit on our vehicles. You can explore as much as you want without worrying about mileage restrictions.

What if I returning vehicle late and extended date of returning.

There will be some amount will be charge from you on returning vehicle late. And if you extend the date and want to continue the ride for few more days then you have to pay the charges as the amount per day, it depends on the number of days.
